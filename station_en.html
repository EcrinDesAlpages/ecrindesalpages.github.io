La Plagne - PARADISKI

A ski resort with a huge number of slopes which offers several activities

The ski area of La Plagne, connected to the ski areas of Les Arcs and Peisey-Vallandry, is one of the largest ski domains in France! The combined ski slopes of all three resorts form Paradiski, a "super domain" that lives up to its name. Indeed, Paradiski is a Ski Paradise for all snow sports enthusiasts: 425 km of green, blue, red, and black slopes that will appeal to beginners as well as advanced and even expert skiers (alpine skiing, freestyle, freeride, etc.). Good to know: 70% of the Paradiski ski area is located at altitudes of over 2000 meters. Snow coverage on the slopes is therefore guaranteed during your winter and spring holidays in La Plagne.

Children are not forgotten
